AI Summaries turn last week's mentions into a short, readable brief so you can zoom out from individual mentions and spot gaps and wins fast.

<Info>
AI Summaries are included on the **Scale** plan.
</Info>

## What you get

Each week you receive:

- **Your brand summary:** everything tracked for your brand
- **A competitor summary:** for the competitors you track

## What shows up each week

- **Volume and trend by source:** what moved, and where
- **Highlights and lowlights:** the biggest wins and issues
- **Sentiment change:** week-over-week movement
- **Posts used as sources:** so you can click through to the original posts

## How to use it

We set up the summaries automatically. Open the **"AI Summaries"** page in Octolens to see current and past summaries.

You'll get email alerts by default, but you can also set them up as [Slack alerts](/quickstart/connect-slack).

You may want to:

- Open the linked source posts that deserve a response.
- Share the brief in your weekly team update.

<Accordion title="How we decide what matters">
We use the same AI relevance, tags, and sentiment as your feed. See [AI Relevance & Tags](/learn/ai-relevance) and [Sentiment](/learn/sentiment) for more details.
</Accordion>
