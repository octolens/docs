title: AI Relevance & Tags
description: How we analyze and tag your posts
---

We want to notify you about the posts you truly care about. That is why we use AI to assess a post's relevance to your business and tag it, so you can filter.

When a post comes in mentioning one of your keywords:

- We use your company context to check if it is related
- If it's not, we mark it as "irrelevant"
- If it is relevant, we categorize it with one or more of the following tags:
  - Own brand mention
  - Competitor mention
  - Industry insight
  - Buy intent
  - Bug report
  - User feedback
  - Promotional post
  - Product question
  - Event
  - Hiring
