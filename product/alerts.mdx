You can create a notification based on any of your saved views. We have a list of default views, and you can create your own. Once a view is set up, you can enable it as a notification in the top-right of the screen. You can also set one up from your notifications page.

Once you enter the notification setup flow, here are the steps:

### Step 1: Choose Notification Settings

- Choose the view you want to get notified for in the dropdown (if the view doesn't exist yet, create it first)
- Check over the filters of that view including:
  - Keyword
  - Platform
  - Tag
  - Sentiment
  - Language
- You can also set advanced filters.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/alerts-step1-settings.png" alt="Step 1: Choose notification settings" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Select destination and Frequency

Choose where you want to send your notification. You can choose between:

- Email
- Slack notification (make sure your Slack workspace is connected first)
- Webhook

Choose the frequency of the notification.
