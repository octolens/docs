Ever stumbled on a social post mentioning your product, your competitors, or the problem you're solving? Wouldn't it be great if you could get notified when someone mentions you on X, Hacker News, GitHub, Reddit, podcasts, etc.?

## Introducing Octolens

Octolens is an AI-powered social listening tool that monitors keyword mentions across [relevant platforms for B2B SaaS](/learn/platforms/overview). It helps monitor brand mentions, competitor activity, and keyword discussions, providing alerts and insights to engage in relevant conversations.

Octolens comes with AI-driven post assessment to prioritize actionable insights. It filters out noise and focuses on what matters to your brand.

For example, by tracking your brand, industry terms, and competitors, you can find posts like:

- **Brand love** — positive mentions and testimonials about your product
- **Buy intent** — people searching for tool recommendations in your space
- **Product questions** — users asking about your product or features
- **Competitor complaints** — frustrations with competitors you can address
- **Pain points** — problems your product can help solve

For more help on keywords, check out [Choosing Keywords](/quickstart/keywords).
