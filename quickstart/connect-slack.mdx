---
title: Connect Slack
description: Connect your Slack workspace to receive Octolens notifications
---

Octolens integrates with Slack so your team can receive mention alerts directly in a Slack channel. Before you can send notifications to Slack, you need to connect your Slack workspace.

## How to connect Slack

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings in Octolens">
    Navigate to **Settings** in your Octolens workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Integrations or Notifications tab">
    Find the Slack integration section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Slack">
    You'll be redirected to Slack to authorize the Octolens app for your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a channel">
    Select the Slack channel where you want to receive notifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once your workspace is connected, you can select **Slack** as a destination when [setting up alerts](/quickstart/alerts).

## What you can send to Slack

You can send any view-based notification to Slack. This means you control exactly which mentions reach your team. For example:

- Send only **High Relevance** mentions to a dedicated channel
- Send **Buy Intent** posts to your sales team's channel
- Send **Crisis Management** alerts to a leadership channel

You can also choose which **relevance levels** to include. For example, get notified only for posts the AI deems as high relevance (buy-intent, engagement opportunities, customer complaints, or praise).

<Info>
Make sure your Slack workspace is connected **before** creating a Slack notification. If Slack isn't connected, the Slack option won't appear as a destination in the alert setup flow.
</Info>

Once connected, head to [Alerts](/quickstart/alerts) to create your first Slack notification.
