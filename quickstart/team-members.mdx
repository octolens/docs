title: Team Members
description: Invite teammates to your Octolens workspace
---

You can add teammates on all Octolens plans so your whole team can collaborate on social listening.

There are two roles available in Octolens: **Admin** and **Member**. Admins can manage billing, invite members, assign admin roles, and create admin-level API keys. Members can access the workspace but cannot manage billing or admin-level settings.

## How to invite a teammate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Members" />
  <Step title="Add their email address" />
  <Step title="They'll receive an invitation to join your workspace" />
</Steps>

Once added, teammates can enter your workspace and collaborate. They'll have access to your keywords, views, and mentions feed.

<Info>
All Octolens plans have unlimited seats. There's no need to upgrade to invite your teammates.
</Info>
