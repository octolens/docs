You can export your Octolens mentions data as a CSV file for offline analysis, reporting, or importing into other tools.

## How to export

In the top right of any open feed, click **Export**. The export will include all mentions matching the currently applied filters in that feed.

This means you can use [feeds](/quickstart/feeds) to narrow down exactly what you want to export by keyword, platform, sentiment, tag, or any other filter, and then export just that subset.

For programmatic access or large/repeated exports, use the [API](/api/v2/overview) or [MCP server](/mcp/overview) instead.
