You can export your Octolens mentions data as a CSV file for offline analysis, reporting, or importing into other tools.

## How to export

In the top right of any open view, click **CSV Export**. The export will include all mentions matching the currently applied filters in that view.

This means you can use [views](/quickstart/views) to narrow down exactly what you want to export — by keyword, platform, sentiment, tag, or any other filter — and then export just that subset.
