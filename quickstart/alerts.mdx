Alerts take mentions that match a [view](/quickstart/views) and push them somewhere — email, Slack, or a webhook — at the frequency you choose. An alert is always:

**a view + one or more destinations + a frequency.**

One alert can fan out to multiple destinations at once (for example, email + Slack + webhook).

## The Alerts page

Manage every alert in your workspace from **My alerts** in the sidebar — or directly at [app.octolens.com/me/notifications](https://app.octolens.com/me/notifications).

<Frame caption="The Alerts page — one row per alert, with the linked view, destination, and frequency.">
  <img src="/images/alerts-page.png" alt="Alerts page showing a list of alerts with View and Destination and frequency columns and an Add alert button" />
</Frame>

Each row shows:

- **View** — the saved view that feeds this alert
- **Destination & frequency** — where alerts go (e.g. a Slack channel like `octolens-buy-intent`) and how often (`Instant`, `Daily`, etc.)
- A **···** menu to **Edit** or **Delete** the alert

## Create an alert

You can start the setup from two places:

- **From any view**, click **Set up alert** in the top-right.

<Frame caption="The Set up alert button in the top-right of any view.">
  <img src="/images/alerts-feed-setup.png" alt="View header showing Export and Set up alert buttons" />
</Frame>

- **From the Alerts page**, click **+ Add alert** in the top-right.

Either way, the Add alert dialog walks you through two steps.

### Step 1: Choose alert settings

Pick the **Saved view** that will feed this alert. The alert will fire whenever a new mention matches that view's filters.

<Frame caption="Step 1 — pick the view that feeds the alert.">
  <img src="/images/alerts-step1-settings.png" alt="Add alert dialog Step 1 with a Saved view dropdown" />
</Frame>

If the view doesn't exist yet, close the dialog, [create it in Views](/quickstart/views), then start the alert setup again.

### Step 2: Select destination & frequency

Toggle on one or more destinations and configure each one. You can turn on email + Slack + webhook simultaneously — they all fire off the same view.

<Frame caption="Step 2 — enable one or more destinations.">
  <img src="/images/alerts-step2-destination.png" alt="Add alert dialog Step 2 with Email notification, Slack notification, and Webhook toggles" />
</Frame>

#### Email

<Frame caption="Email alert config — one or more recipients, plus a delivery frequency.">
  <img src="/images/alerts-email.png" alt="Email alert panel with email recipients input, Add recipient button, and Frequency radio buttons" />
</Frame>

- **Email recipient(s)** — enter one email per field. Click **+ Add recipient** to send to multiple addresses.
- **Frequency** — Instantly, Hourly, Daily, or Weekly. Hourly, Daily, and Weekly are batched — you get one digest per interval with all matching mentions.

#### Slack

<Frame caption="Slack alert config — one or more channels, plus a delivery frequency.">
  <img src="/images/alerts-slack.png" alt="Slack alert panel with Slack channels multi-select and Frequency radio buttons" />
</Frame>

- **Slack channel(s)** — multi-select the channels you want the alert to post to.
- **Frequency** — same options as email, with batched delivery for Hourly/Daily/Weekly.

Slack requires a connected workspace. See [Connect Slack](/quickstart/connect-slack) if the Slack toggle isn't available.

#### Webhook

<Frame caption="Webhook config — one URL, plus a delivery frequency and a Test button.">
  <img src="/images/alerts-webhook.png" alt="Webhook panel with Webhook URL input, Test button, and Frequency radio buttons" />
</Frame>

- **Webhook URL** — the endpoint Octolens will POST to.
- **Test** — send a sample payload to your endpoint to verify it's reachable before you save.
- **Frequency** — Instantly, Hourly, Daily, or Weekly.

For payload format, signature verification, and retry behavior, see [Webhooks](/quickstart/webhooks).

## Frequency explained

All three destinations support the same four frequencies:

- **Instantly** — each matching mention is sent as soon as Octolens ingests it
- **Hourly** — one batched delivery per hour with all matches from that hour
- **Daily** — one batched delivery per day
- **Weekly** — one batched delivery per week

<Tip>
Use **Instantly** for time-sensitive views like **Crisis Management** and **Buy Intent** where minutes matter. Use a batched frequency for noisier views (e.g. **Industry insights**) so you're not interrupted all day.
</Tip>

## Edit or delete an alert

On the Alerts page, click the **···** menu next to any alert:

- **Edit** — change the view, destinations, recipients, channels, webhook URL, or frequency
- **Delete** — remove the alert. This can't be undone. Deleting doesn't affect the underlying view — just stops deliveries.
