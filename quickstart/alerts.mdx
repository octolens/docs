title: Alerts
description: Get alerts via email, Slack, or webhook
---

You can create alerts based on any of your saved [views](/quickstart/views). Once a view is set up, you can enable it as an alert. Octolens supports three alert destinations: **Email**, **Slack**, and **Webhook**.

## How to set up an alert

You can start the alert setup from two places:

**From your feed** — click "Enable as alert" in the top-right of any view.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/alerts-feed-setup.png" alt="Set up alerts from the feed" />
</Frame>

**From the [alerts page](https://octolens.com/me/notifications)** — manage all your alerts in one place.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/alerts-notifications-page.png" alt="Set up alerts from the alerts page" />
</Frame>

### Step 1: Choose alert settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose the view you want to get alerted for">
    Select a view from the dropdown. If the view doesn't exist yet, [create it first](/quickstart/views).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the filters of that view">
    Review the filters including **Keyword**, **Platform**, **Tag**, **Sentiment**, and **Language**. You can also set advanced filters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/alerts-step1-settings.png" alt="Step 1: Choose alert settings" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Select destination and frequency

Choose where you want to send your alert and how often.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/alerts-step2-destination.png" alt="Step 2: Select destination and frequency" />
</Frame>

## Email alerts

Select **Email** as your destination. You'll receive mention alerts directly in your inbox at the frequency you choose.

## Slack alerts

Select **Slack** as your destination. Make sure your [Slack workspace is connected](/quickstart/connect-slack) first—otherwise the Slack option won't appear.

You can choose which relevance levels to include. For example, get alerted only for posts the AI deems as high relevance (buy-intent, engagement opportunities, customer complaints, or praise).

## Webhook alerts

Select **Webhook** as your destination. This lets you send mention data to any external app. For full details on webhook setup and payload format, see [Webhooks](/quickstart/webhooks).
