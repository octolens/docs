Octolens is a social listening tool for developer and B2B SaaS brands. It monitors mentions across [13+ platforms](/learn/platforms/overview) — including Reddit, X, LinkedIn, Hacker News, GitHub, YouTube, Bluesky, TikTok, podcasts, and newsletters — and uses AI to filter out noise so you only see mentions that matter.

</Frame>

## What you can do with Octolens

Track your brand, competitors, and industry terms to find posts like:

- **Brand love** — positive mentions and testimonials about your product
- **Buy intent** — people searching for tool recommendations in your space
- **Product questions** — users asking about your product or features
- **Competitor complaints** — frustrations with competitors you can address
- **Pain points** — problems your product can help solve

Each mention comes with an AI-assigned [sentiment](/learn/sentiment), [relevance score, and tags](/learn/ai-relevance), plus a one-line explanation of why it matched your keyword.

## What's in the app

- **Feed** — every mention across every platform, organized into [views](/quickstart/views)
- **Bookmarks** — mentions you've saved to come back to
- **Analytics** — trends, conversation ranks, and mention volume over time. See [Analytics](/learn/analytics).
- **My alerts** — email, Slack, and webhook alerts. See [Alerts](/quickstart/alerts).
- **API / MCP** — generate API keys and MCP URLs. See [API Reference](/api/overview) and [MCP Server](/mcp/overview).
- **Settings** — company context, keywords, team members, integrations, and billing
- **Keywords** — the list of keywords you're tracking. Click any keyword to filter the feed.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your company context">
    Tell Octolens about your business so the AI can assess mentions accurately. This is the single biggest lever on relevance quality.
    [Set up company context →](/quickstart/company-context)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your keywords">
    Add your brand name, competitors, and industry terms. Start with 3–5 keywords — you can always add more.
    [Choose keywords →](/quickstart/keywords)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize mentions with views">
    Views are saved filters over your feed — think "Buy intent this week" or "Competitor mentions on Reddit." Octolens ships with presets like For you, Brand monitoring, and Competitor intelligence.
    [Set up views →](/quickstart/views)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up alerts">
    Get alerted by email, Slack, or webhook when new mentions match a view.
    [Set up alerts →](/quickstart/alerts)
  </Step>
  <Step title="(Optional) Connect to your stack">
    Pull mentions into your own tools via [API](/api/overview), [webhooks](/quickstart/webhooks), or [MCP](/mcp/overview). All three are available on every plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>
