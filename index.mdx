Octolens is a social listening tool for developer and B2B SaaS brands. It monitors mentions across [13+ platforms](/learn/platforms/overview), including Reddit, X, LinkedIn, Hacker News, GitHub, YouTube, Bluesky, TikTok, podcasts, and newsletters. AI filters out the noise so you only see mentions that matter.

## What you can do with Octolens

Track your brand, competitors, and industry terms to find posts like:

- **Brand love:** positive mentions and testimonials about your product
- **Buy intent:** people searching for tool recommendations in your space
- **Product questions:** users asking about your product or features
- **Competitor complaints:** frustrations with competitors you can address
- **Pain points:** problems your product can help solve

Each mention comes with an AI-assigned [sentiment](/learn/sentiment), [relevance score, and tags](/learn/ai-relevance), plus a one-line explanation of why it matched your keyword.

## What's in the app

- **Dashboard:** mentions over time, plan and API/MCP usage, and a recent mentions feed. See [Dashboard](/learn/dashboard).
- **Keywords:** the dedicated page where you add, edit, and tune every keyword you track, plus global keyword settings. See [Keywords](/quickstart/keywords).
- **Feeds:** saved filters over your mentions, like Brand Mentions, Competitor Mentions, or Buy Intent. Each feed can be sent to a [destination](/quickstart/destinations). See [Feeds](/quickstart/feeds).
- **API:** generate API keys and use the REST API to query mentions, keywords, and feeds. See [API Reference](/api/v2/overview).
- **MCP:** connect Octolens to Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, and other AI tools to ask questions of your mention data in natural language. See [MCP Server](/mcp/overview).
- **Settings:** company context, plans and billing, members, platforms, and the Slack integration.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your company context">
    Tell Octolens about your business so the AI can assess mentions accurately. This is the single biggest lever on relevance quality.
    [Set up company context →](/quickstart/company-context)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your keywords">
    Add your brand name, competitors, and industry terms. Start with 3 to 5 keywords. You can always add more.
    [Choose keywords →](/quickstart/keywords)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize mentions into feeds">
    Feeds are saved filters over your mentions. Think "Buy intent this week" or "Competitor mentions on Reddit." Octolens ships with presets like Brand Mentions, Competitor Mentions, and Buy Intent.
    [Set up feeds →](/quickstart/feeds)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send feeds to a destination">
    Pick where each feed goes: email, Slack, or a webhook. One feed can fan out to all three.
    [Set up destinations →](/quickstart/destinations)
  </Step>
  <Step title="(Optional) Connect to your stack">
    Pull mentions into your own tools and AI agents via [MCP](/mcp/overview), [API](/api/v2/overview), or [webhooks](/quickstart/webhooks). All three are available on every plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>
